DreamMixer – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-09

This Privacy Policy explains how the DreamMixer app handles user data.

1. What data do we process?

  • App functionality data: settings and preferences (e.g., volumes, favorites, timer). These are stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising-related data (if you are not PRO): advertising SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers and ad/interactions data to display and measure ads.
  • In-app purchases / subscription: subscriptions are handled through Google Play. DreamMixer does not see or store your payment details; Google Play processes them.

2. How do we use data?

  • To operate core app features (mix, favorites, timer).
  • To show and measure ads for non-PRO users.
  • To unlock PRO features via Google Play subscription status.

3. Third-party services

DreamMixer may use third-party services for certain features (e.g., ads, measurement, subscription handling), such as:

  • Google Play Billing (subscriptions / purchases)
  • Google AdMob (ads for non-PRO users)

4. Data storage

  • Local storage: preferences are stored on your device (e.g., SharedPreferences).
  • Advertising data: ad providers may process data on their systems under their own policies.

5. Children

The app is intended for a general audience. If your target audience includes children under 13, you must configure the appropriate settings in Google Play Console.

6. Data deletion request

If you would like us to delete the data you provided, please send your request to: myhapyapp@gmail.com

7. Contact

Contact email: myhapyapp@gmail.com

Note: If new data processing is added later (e.g., analytics, login), this policy must be updated.
